About Us

The men of Tau Kappa Epsilon-Beta Theta strive to achieve academically while staying actively involved within our community and the University of Missouri's Greek life. We compete in both Homecoming and Greek Week, consistently participate in other sororities’ philanthropy/charity efforts, and compete in fraternal intramural sport leagues offered by the University of Missouri.

Philanthropy

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital

TKE's Make A Change

Tau Kappa Epsilon's nationwide charity is focsued on St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, founded by TKE brother Danny Thomas. TKE officially joined the fight against childhood cancer with St. Jude Children's Research Hospital in 1978. And 40 years later, TKE still continues to support this amazing cause.

Social

Tau Kappa Epsilon hosts socials in order to get better acquainted with the lovely sororities that populate The University of Missouri's Greek life. We plan a marriage and divorce party each semester intending to celebrate the start and successful run of our pairing in conjunction with Homecoming and Greek week.

The Beta Theta chapter organizes our Red Carnation Ball annually every spring to where we hold an initial election for the location of our formal event. Each member invites a date to our formal dinner ceremony where members are awarded for their individual merits. These awards for chapter excellence include most outstanding new member and the three cardinal principles of Tau Kappa Epsilon - love, charity, and esteem.
